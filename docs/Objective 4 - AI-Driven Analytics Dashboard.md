# Objective 4: AI-Driven Analytics Dashboard using Gemini AI API

**Owner (Member Name):** Guyala, Jhon Jerick  
**Objective #:** 4  

## Objective Description
The AI-Driven Analytics Dashboard covers the basic KPIs including the total sales, expenses, profit, and profit margin. Apart from this, the AI-integration of this module covers forecasting of sales and expenses, and product trends, also the actionable recommendations for sales optimization. Additionally, this module matters to the overall capstone project as it highlights the AI-driven integration and will also benefit the owner in terms of decision-making process. Lastly, it connects to other members' objectives by getting the data from the online ordering, POS system, and stock inventory management of the system.
